# Acceptable Use Policy

**Docs version**: 2026.05.15<br>
**Last reviewed**: 2026-05-15<br>
**Scope**: Authorized use boundaries for Armageddon testing tooling

Armageddon tooling is authorized only for systems you own or systems where you have explicit written permission to test. Unauthorized use is prohibited and may violate applicable law.

## Permitted Use

- Local simulation and Moat-contained validation against owned systems.
- Security, resilience, and quality testing under a written authorization boundary.
- Internal product demonstrations that use simulated targets or approved sandbox tenants.

## Prohibited Use

- Testing third-party systems without written authorization.
- Attempting to bypass production safety controls, rate limits, circuit breakers, or authentication checks.
- Capturing, storing, or sharing secrets outside approved secure channels.
- Running destructive workflows without `SIM_MODE` and sandbox tenant controls.

APEX Business Systems Ltd. accepts no liability for unauthorized use.
